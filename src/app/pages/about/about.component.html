About

Your streaming companion for movies, TV shows, and anime.

What We Do

PopAuraStream is a modern streaming companion that helps you discover, track, and organize your favorite movies and TV shows. We bring together content from multiple sources into one seamless experience.

Fast & Responsive

Built with performance in mind. Instant search, smooth animations, and optimized performance across all devices — from desktop to mobile.

Privacy First

No accounts required. Your watch history, favorites, and lists are stored locally in your browser. PopAuraStream only uses a lightweight visitor counter to show total visits.

Open Source

PopAuraStream is built with Angular and is completely open source. Contributions, feedback, and community involvement are always welcome.
